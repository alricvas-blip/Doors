Analysing Typical Thickness Graph

The first graph illustrates the typical thickness of nine door types, showing a range between 37.5 mm and 42.5 mm. Most categories, including Flush, Glass, Bi-fold, Sliding, and Pocket doors, maintain a thickness of 37.5 mm, while Panel, French, and Double doors are slightly thicker at 40 mm. The Personal Access door is the thickest variety shown at 42.5 mm.
